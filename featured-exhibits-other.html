More Info

Redfox Technologies (Ph)

A Philippine technology brand known for affordable computing solutions and consumer electronics.

Year: 1988

More Info

Redfox Technologies became well known for providing local computing solutions, competing with global brands by offering cost-effective alternatives in the Philippine market.

Tower Records Makati

A popular music retail store in the Philippines, known for selling CDs, vinyl, and cassette tapes.

Year: 1990s - Mid 2000s

More Info

Tower Records Makati was a go-to destination for music lovers, offering a vast collection of physical media formats. It became a cultural hub before the decline of physical music retail.

CD-R King Shop

An electronics store offering affordable tech accessories, active from the late '90s to 2020.

Year: 1997-2020

More Info

CD-R King was known for offering budget-friendly tech products, from blank CDs to peripherals. It became a staple in local electronics retail until its closure in 2020.

MyPhone

The first local mobile brand in the Philippines, introducing dual-SIM technology.

Year: 2007

More Info

MyPhone revolutionized the Philippine mobile market by offering affordable dual-SIM phones, catering to users who needed multiple network options.

Cherry Mobile

Launched in 2009, introduced budget smartphones with multiple SIMs and later partnered with Google for Android One in 2015.

Year: 2009

More Info

Cherry Mobile revolutionized budget-friendly mobile devices in the Philippines, introducing cost-effective smartphones with innovative SIM technology. Its partnership with Google for Android One solidified its presence in the global market.
