1980s

The Rise of Personal Computing

Video courtesy of YouTube Channel: SQRtv.

IBM launches the first IBM PC (1981), revolutionizing personal computing.

Apple introduces the Macintosh (1984), bringing graphical user interfaces to the mainstream.

CD-ROM technology emerges, enabling digital storage and multimedia applications (1985).

1990s

The Internet Boom

Video courtesy of YouTube Channel: ViceTV.

The World Wide Web is invented by Tim Berners-Lee (1991), changing global communication.

Microsoft releases Windows 95 (1995), introducing the Start Menu and Plug & Play.

Google is founded (1998), setting the stage for modern search engines.

2000s

Mobile & Social Media Revolution

Video courtesy of YouTube Channel: CBC.

Apple launches the iPod (2001), transforming digital music consumption.

Facebook is founded (2004), marking the rise of social networking.

The first iPhone is released (2007), revolutionizing smartphones.

2010s

AI & Cloud Computing

Video courtesy of YouTube Channel: Hitechies Your Startup Tech Insights .

Cloud computing becomes mainstream with services like AWS and Google Cloud (2010s).

AI-powered assistants like Siri and Alexa emerge (2011-2014).

Blockchain technology gains traction with Bitcoin (2010s).

2020s

The Era of AI & Quantum Computing

Video courtesy of YouTube Channel: AI Uncovered.

Artificial Intelligence transforms industries with advanced automation, decision-making, and content generation (2020s).

Quantum Computing accelerates breakthroughs in cryptography, simulations, and optimization, reshaping computing power (2020s).
